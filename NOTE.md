# 📜 Divine Intervention & Architectural Ascendance

### A Note on the "AI-Enhanced" Abstraction Layer

Look, let's be real for a second. This was supposed to be a small project, a "quick weekend warrior" kind of deal. I had every intention of hand-crafting every single byte of this console while sipping artisanal coffee. But then reality hit: I realized that manually managing multiple output streams, file pointers, and dynamic color parsing is exactly the kind of repetitive work that makes me want to take a permanent nap.

Being a man of high standards (and strategic laziness), I decided that instead of fighting the machine, I would command it. 

I utilized **AI** to architect the unified abstraction layer (`write_to_outway`) and the stream-monitoring system. Why? Because I wanted a project that is actually scalable, robust, and doesn't crash the moment you look at it funny. It’s not "cheating"—it’s **Advanced Engineering Delegation™**.

Everything else in this repository was, of course, generated by the Grace of God, channeled directly through my keyboard for your benefit.
